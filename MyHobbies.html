Elio has a passion for the sport of Football, known as soccer in North America. Elio has played football since he was a young boy, and has always had love for the sport. Throughout his upbringing, Elio joined multiple teams and saw his game improve immensely as he grew up.In terms of watching the sport, Elio is a big fan of Arsenal Football Club, with his favorite player there being Gabriel Martinelli. Arsenal Football Club Currently, Elio plays football at an intramural level at the University of Ottawa, where he plays with his friends in a team called Ensay F.C.

Another hobby of Elio's is playing video games, specificically Call of Duty Warzone. Warzone for short, is a shooting game in a battle royale format, where 150 players are put on the a battle field and must kill one another to come out on top. Elio has played almost every Call of Duty since 2009, starting at Modern Warfare 2, and has been a fan of the series since. As recent as Apirl, Elio has been playing Warzone to the level of addiction, trying constantly to improve while having fun with his friends.
